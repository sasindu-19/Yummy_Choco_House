Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click the "Buy Now" button on the item you like, and you'll be redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Just send the message and we'll guide you through the rest of the ordering process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and cash on delivery within Colombo. Payment details will be provided after confirming your order through WhatsApp.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery throughout Sri Lanka. Delivery within Colombo is Rs. 300, and outside Colombo varies based on location. Exact delivery charges will be confirmed during order placement.

Can I customize my chocolate box?

Yes! We offer customization options for most of our products. Just let us know your preferences when chatting with us on WhatsApp, and we'll do our best to create exactly what you're looking for.

How long do deliveries take?

Deliveries within Colombo typically take 1-2 days. Outside Colombo may take 2-3 days depending on the location. For urgent orders, please mention this when placing your order and we'll try our best to accommodate your timeline.
