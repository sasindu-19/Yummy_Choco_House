Our Sweet Story

Founded in 2018, Yummy Choco House began as a small home kitchen in Colombo with a passion for creating delicious chocolates that blend traditional Sri Lankan flavors with premium ingredients.

Our journey from a small family business to Sri Lanka's beloved chocolate brand has been filled with love, dedication, and the pursuit of chocolate perfection. Every piece is handcrafted with care and attention to detail.
